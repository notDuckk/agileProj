More details:

The Clefairy Pokémon card is part of the original Base Set, released by Wizards of the Coast in 1999. It features Clefairy, a Fairy-type Pokémon, depicted as a Normal type in the early sets. The card's artwork, created by Mitsuhiro Arita, showcases Clefairy standing in a night setting with stars in the background. It has a holographic foil treatment, making it one of the more visually appealing cards in the set. Clefairy has 40 HP, with "Sing" and "Metronome" as its two moves. "Sing" can put the opponent's Pokémon to sleep, while "Metronome" allows Clefairy to copy any of the defending Pokémon's moves. As a collectible, the card holds significant nostalgic and monetary value, especially in mint condition. It is also notable for evolving into Clefable, another Pokémon from the original 151.
