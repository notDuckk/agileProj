The Arceus Pokémon card is a legendary and mythical card in the Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG), often depicted as a Colorless-type Pokémon with extraordinary powers. It typically boasts high HP and versatile attacks, representing its status as the creator of the Pokémon universe. Arceus cards often feature unique gameplay mechanics, such as its ability to change types or utilize powerful signature moves like "Judgment." The card artwork often showcases Arceus in majestic and awe-inspiring poses, emphasizing its divine nature. Due to its rarity and legendary status, Arceus cards are highly coveted by collectors and players alike, often commanding high values in the TCG market.
