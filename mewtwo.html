The Mewtwo Pokémon card is a prominent card in the Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG). Mewtwo, a powerful Psychic-type Pokémon, first appeared in the Base Set released in 1999. The card features artwork of Mewtwo in a dynamic pose, usually using or preparing to use a Psychic attack. With a HP (Hit Points) of 60, its moves often include "Psychic" and "Barrier." Mewtwo cards are known for their strong offensive capabilities and strategic importance in gameplay. The Base Set Mewtwo is especially valued for its rarity and nostalgia. Various versions of Mewtwo cards have been released across different TCG sets, including promo editions and special holographic prints, making it a favorite among collectors and competitive players alike.
